Design Challenge: Make Indoor Gardening Easy & Fun


Background

Grove was based on an idea: if people grow some of the food they eat, they'll live healthier and move fulfilling lives.

The company's goal was to get people growing their own food, and the company's strategy was threefold:

  1. Build beautiful, easy-to-use kitchen gardening appliances to automate the tedious parts of the gardening experience.
  2. Deliver ready-to-grow "seedpods" that could be inserted into the appliacne like a Kureig cup.
  3. Tie these together with a software experience that combines a mobile app with a backend API service capable of controlling the appliances remotely and learning over time how to best grow each pod.
The App

The app was designed to deliver on three core promises:

  1. Allow the user to set up & control their appliance hardware.
  2. Give users guidance on planting, maintaining, harvesting, and eating their garden.
  3. Offer users a porthole to manage their "Grove Experience" by customizing their subscription of seedpods.
